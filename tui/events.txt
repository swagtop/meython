round initiation:
'b' = beginning of round

bot responses:
'r' = rolled
'l' = lift
'a' = above or equal the previous
's' = say roll

specifying:
'c' = current bot
'p' = previous bot 
'd' = takes damage
'w' = wins game

damage reasons:
't' = too low
'f' = failed lift
'u' = untrue answer
'i' = invalid response
'e' = answer raised exception

amount:
'n' = normal amount
'm' = meyer amount

in cup:
' 21' = when the cup is lifted, the
        contents are revealed at the
        end of the string, separated 
        from the rest of the events
        by a single space
